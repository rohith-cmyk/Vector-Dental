## 11. Login / Signup Flow (from Header)

### Login
- **Log In** → Modal or page: "Choose your portal"
  - **General Dentist** → Redirect to gd.vectorreferral.com/login
  - **Specialist** → Redirect to specialist.vectorreferral.com/login

### Signup
- **Start for Free** → Same wizard as specialist signup:
  1. Office name
  2. Office email
  3. Office phone
  4. **General Dentist or Specialist?** → GD → gd.vectorreferral.com/signup; Specialist → Step 5
  5. Full name, password → Create account
