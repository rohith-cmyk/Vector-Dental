Vector Dental - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Vector Dental (“Vector Dental,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is a dental referral and care-coordination platform operated by R&D Venture Cohort 1, Inc. We provide software tools that help dental practices securely send referrals, coordinate care, and communicate with patients.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when clinics and patients interact with our services.

1. Who We Are

Vector Dental is a technology platform used by dental practices. We are not a healthcare provider and do not provide medical or dental treatment.

  • Operator: R&D Venture Cohort 1, Inc.
  • Support Email: support@rdventurestudios.com
  • Service Area: United States

2. Information We Collect

Information Provided by Dental Practices

Dental clinics using Vector Dental may enter patient and referral information into our platform, including:

  • Patient name
  • Phone number
  • Referral details
  • Appointment or scheduling information
  • Notes related to the referral

Clinics are responsible for obtaining patient consent before entering information into the platform.

Automatically Collected Information

When users access our services, we may collect:

  • IP address
  • Device and browser type
  • Log files
  • Usage activity for security and performance monitoring

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Facilitate dental referrals between clinics
  • Send referral and appointment notifications
  • Deliver scheduling links and status updates
  • Provide platform functionality
  • Maintain security and prevent fraud
  • Improve and operate our services

We do not use patient phone numbers for marketing or promotional purposes.

4. SMS Text Messaging Disclosures

When a patient’s phone number is provided to Vector Dental by a clinic, the patient may receive SMS messages related to their dental referral or appointment.

SMS Messages May Include:

  • Referral notifications
  • Scheduling links
  • Appointment reminders
  • Care-related status updates

Consent

General dentists obtain explicit patient consent before submitting a phone number through Vector Dental. Consent is recorded in the platform prior to sending SMS messages.

Important SMS Terms:

  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • Message frequency varies.
  • To opt out at any time, reply STOP.
  • For help, reply HELP or contact support@rdventurestudios.com.

No Sale or Sharing of Opt-In Data

Opt-in data and consent for SMS communications are not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. How We Share Information

We may share information only as necessary to operate our services, including with:

  • SMS delivery providers (such as telecommunications platforms)
  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Security and infrastructure vendors

These providers are authorized to use information solely to perform services on our behalf.

We do not sell personal information.

6. Data Security

We maintain administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

7. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide services, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

8. Children’s Privacy

Vector Dental does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without appropriate consent from a parent or guardian.

9. Your Rights and Choices

Patients may:

  • Opt out of SMS messages by replying STOP
  • Contact the dental clinic that submitted their referral
  • Contact us at support@rdventurestudios.com with questions

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted publicly with a revised “Last Updated” date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact:

Email: support@rdventurestudios.com
